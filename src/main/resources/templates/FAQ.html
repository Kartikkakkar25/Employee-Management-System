Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Employee Management System (EMS)?

The EMS is a web-based application that helps organizations manage employee data efficiently.

Who can access the Employee Management System?

Only registered users with valid login credentials can access the system.

How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page or contact the administrator.

Why is my username showing as ‘null’ after logging in?

This may be due to session issues. Try refreshing the page or logging out and back in.

How do I view employee details?

If you are an Admin, Go to the "Employees" section in the navigation menu to see the list of all employees.

Can I change my username?

No, usernames are assigned by the admin and cannot be changed.

Is my data secure?

Yes, the system uses Spring Security to protect user data and enforce access control.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized access to my account?

Immediately reset your password and report the issue to the admin.
